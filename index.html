How It Works

1. Project Analysis

Our expert analysts identify and evaluate high-potential investment opportunities using advanced market research and predictive algorithms.

2. Investment Matching

We match investors with projects that align with their risk profile and investment goals, ensuring optimal alignment of interests.

3. Active Management

For qualified projects, we actively manage the investment, providing expertise and oversight to maximize success potential.

4. Profit Distribution

Once projects succeed, profits are distributed according to our transparent model - 80% to investors, 20% to I&S Society.

Our Profit Model

For Investors

80%

When you invest through I&S Society, you receive 80% of the profits from successful projects. We handle all the research and management while you enjoy the majority of returns.

For I&S Society

20%

We take a 20% share of profits from successful investments as compensation for our research, analysis, and project matching services.

Project Management

30%

For projects we actively manage, we receive 30% of profits after recovering our capital. If a project doesn't succeed initially, we continue investing until it does.
